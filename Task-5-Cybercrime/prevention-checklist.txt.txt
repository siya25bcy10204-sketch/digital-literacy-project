Prevention Checklist (Stay Safe Online)
* Always check the website URL before entering login details.
* Never share OTP or passwords with anyone.
* Use strong and unique passwords.
* Enable two-factor authentication.
* Avoid clicking suspicious email links.
* Verify unknown messages before responding.
* Use secure networks while making online payments.
* Regularly update antivirus software.
UPI Safety
* Always verify the receiver's UPI ID before payment.
* Never approve unknown payment requests.
Report cybercrime at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
