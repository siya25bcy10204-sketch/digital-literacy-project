# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
## Do's
*  Think before posting anything online.
*  Use respectful and polite language while communicating.
*  Protect your personal information and privacy.
*  Verify information before sharing it.
*  Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
* ## Don'ts
*  Do not share fake news or unverified information.
*  Do not post offensive or abusive comments.
*  Do not share personal or confidential information publicly.
*  Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
*  Do not click suspicious links from unknown sources.
